# FAQ - Personal AI Assistant

## What is this?
A personal AI that manages your finances — connects to your bank, tracks spending, reminds you of bills, and helps you budget.

## Is my data safe?
Yes. We use Plaid (same as Venmo, Robinhood) which is bank-level security. Your credentials never touch our servers.

## How much does it cost?
**Free during beta.** After launch, likely $9.99/mo for full features.

## What banks work?
Any US bank supported by Plaid — that's most of them (Chase, BofA, Wells Fargo, credit unions, etc.)

## Can it pay my bills?
Not yet — that's the goal. We're starting with tracking and alerts, then adding payment.

## Why is this different from YNAB?
YNAB shows you data. We have an AI that ANSWERS questions and eventually will DO actions. Also — we'll integrate bill PAYMENT.

## How do I join?
Drop a comment in the Discord or DM me. We're starting with 5-10 beta testers.

## What do I need to do?
- Connect your bank (sandbox first = fake data)
- Give feedback on what's useful
- Help shape the product

## Will this work for couples/families?
Planned for later. For now, individual accounts.

## Can I use this for business?
Future feature. Personal first.
