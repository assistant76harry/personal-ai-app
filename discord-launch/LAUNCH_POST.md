## Introducing: Personal AI Assistant

An AI that knows your finances, manages your bills, and helps you make smarter money decisions.

**It's like having a financial advisor in your pocket — but it's YOUR AI, not a company's.**

### What it does NOW:
- 📊 Dashboard showing your real balance
- 💰 Budget tracking with alerts
- 📅 Bill reminders (so you never miss a payment)
- 💬 Chat with your AI about money ("Hey, where am I overspending?")
- 🔗 Bank connection via Plaid (safe, read-only)

### What it WILL do:
- 📱 One app for ALL your finances
- 🤖 AI that actually TAKES action (not just shows data)
- 💸 Pay bills directly (the YNAB gap)
- 📈 Investment tracking
- ✈️ Book travel
- 📧 Auto-categorize bills from email
