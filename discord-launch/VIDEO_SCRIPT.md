# Video Script - Personal AI Assistant

## Hook (3 seconds)
"Imagine if YNAB could actually pay your bills..."

---

## Problem (10 seconds)
"YNAB is great for budgeting. But there's one thing it can't do — actually pay your bills."

"You have to open a separate app. Set up payments. Remember due dates. It's still manual."

---

## Solution (15 seconds)
"What if your AI could do all of it?"

"Connect your bank. See your balance. Get alerts when you're overspending. Ask 'Where did my money go?' and get a real answer."

"And one day... pay your bills with one command?"

"That's what I'm building."

---

## Demo (30 seconds)
[Show app dashboard]
"This is my dashboard right now. See that balance? It's real."

[Show budget]
"Budget tracking — it knows what I'm spending and warns me before I go over."

[Show chat]
"Ask anything — 'What bills are due next week?' 'Am I on track for savings?'"

---

## Call to Action (10 seconds)
"I'm launching it for my community first. Free while testing."

"Drop a comment if you want early access. Let's build this together."

---

## Total: ~68 seconds (perfect for TikTok/Reels/Shorts)

## Alternative - Longer Version (2 min)
Include:
- More features walkthrough
- Community building aspect
- "This is what Siri was supposed to be"

## Filming Tips
- Film vertical (9:16)
- Good lighting on face
- Screen record the app for demo
- Add music overlay (not needed for voice)
